Why the name "Dohl"?

Being 100% honest: The inspiration for the name Dohl comes from the character Doll in the animated show Murder Drones.

We loved the unique sound of the name and wanted to create a helpful AI companion with that same memorable energy. By changing the spelling to Dohl, we've created a version that is focused on being a loyal, intelligent, and educational "Pack Leader" for your information needs.

A Memorable Identity.

We believe in being transparent about our inspirations. Choosing a name from a show we enjoy was our way of making the project personal and fun, while building a tool that is serious about education.
